Introduction

Go to a gloomy and harsh world full of despair. Your fate will depend on the ability to properly assess the situation and build a strategy of behavior, quickly learn and be unpredictable. Dark Souls will require you to be completely concentrated and will severely punish for mistakes. However, when you die, you will become stronger! Each test is a puzzle with an infinite number of solutions. You will need, first of all, the ability to strategically think in order to defeat numerous opponents and complete your path.

Story

In the Age of Ancients, the world was unformed, shrouded by fog. A land of gray crags, Archtrees and Everlasting Dragons. But then there was fire, and with fire came disparity. Heat and cold, life and death, and of course, light and dark. Then from the dark, they came, and found the Souls of Lords within the flame.Nito, the first of the dead,the Witch of Izalith, and her Daughters of Chaos,Gwyn, the Lord of Sunlight, and his faithful knights,and the furtive pygmy,so easily forgotten.
